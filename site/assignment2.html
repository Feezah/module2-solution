Chicken

The chicken is a domesticated junglefowl species, with attributes of wild species such as the grey and the ceylon junglefowl that are originally from Southeastern Asia.

Beef

Beef can be prepared in various ways; cuts are often used for steak, which can be cooked to varying degrees of doneness, while trimmings are often ground or minced, as found in most hamburgers.

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, accompanied by a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, often raw, and vegetables.
